Melisa "Melai" Bonay Cantiveros (born April 6, 1988), is a Filipino actress, comedienne, television personality and former reality show contestant. She is the fifth female Big Winner of the Pinoy Big Brother franchise. She is currently one of ABS-CBN's contract talents. [more]
